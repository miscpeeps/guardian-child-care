# Online Caregiver Search Service

Through Military OneSource, military receive free access to [Sittercity](http://sittercity.com) to help find, hire, and pay for child, pet, or elderly parent care. (waives $100/year subscription fee)

### How to sign up:
1. Log in to your Military OneSource Account. 
2. Member Connect page provides a unique member code, and link to set up the service. Copy the member code.
3. Click on the link to navigate to the child care service page to input your member code, and register your account.

With your account, you can directly login to SitterCity to access resources and tools to search, hire and pay for providers.

[More information and steps here](https://www.militaryonesource.mil/parenting/child-care/military-child-care-services/)
