# Child Care In Your Neighborhood (MCCYN)

MCCYN is program to provide fee assistance for child care in your neighborhood while pending confirmation on a CDC waitlist. 

- [Info for MCCYN and MCCYN Plus fee assistance](https://www.childcareaware.org/fee-assistancerespite/military-families/air-force/fee-assistance/)

MCCYN Plus is pilot program in select locations throughout the country that implements a state Quality Rating and Improvement System for non-accredited, licensed center-based child care programs with the intent to improve the quality of child care. MCCYN-PLUS is only approved if accredited care is not available.Â [More information here](https://www.childcareaware.org/fee-assistancerespite/feeassistancerespiteproviders/qris-mccyn-plus/)

MCCYN Plus is only available in the following areas:
- Colorado Springs, Colorado
- Fayetteville, North Carolina
- Fort Walton Beach, Florida
- Hawaii
- Jacksonville/Mayport, Florida
- Las Vegas, Nevada
- National Capital Region
- Norfolk, Virginia
- San Antonio, Texas
- San Diego, California
- Seattle/Tacoma, Washington
