# Child Care In Your Home (CCYH)

CCYH is a pilot program to provide subsidy for child care in your home while waiting on a CDC waitlist program available in these regions:
- Colorado Springs, Colorado
- Fayetteville, North Carolina
- Fort Walton Beach, Florida
- Hawaii
- Jacksonville/Mayport, Florida
- Las Vegas, Nevada
- National Capital Region
- Norfolk, Virginia
- San Antonio, Texas
- San Diego, California
- Seattle/Tacoma, Washington
